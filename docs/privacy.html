Privacy Policy

Hidden Cats: The Art Gallery
Last Updated: April 2026

1. Overview

This game, "Hidden Cats: The Art Gallery" is a passion project. We do not personally collect, store, or share any of your private information (like names or emails). However, to provide hints and support the game, we use third-party services.

2. Data Collection (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising IDs: Android Ad ID.
  • IP Address: For approximate location services.
  • App Interactions: Analytics on ad views for hint rewards.

3. Third-Party Links

For more details on how your data is handled, please see:

  • Google Play Services Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Policy

4. Children’s Privacy

This game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information. All data collected by third-party SDKs (such as AdMob) is handled securely and encrypted in transit using modern cryptographic protocols like Transport Layer Security (TLS).

6. Your Choices and Data Control

You can opt-out of interest-based advertising or reset your Android Advertising ID at any time through your device settings:

  • Open Settings > Google > Ads.
  • Tap Reset advertising ID or Delete advertising ID.

7. Performance and Diagnostics

Third-party SDKs may collect diagnostic information, such as crash logs and app performance metrics, solely to identify and fix technical issues.
