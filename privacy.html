Data Collection (AdMob) This app uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. Children’s Privacy This game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. 5. Data Security We value your trust in providing us your information. Although this app uses third-party advertising services, we ensure that all data collected by these third-party SDKs (such as AdMob) is handled securely. Your Choices and Data Control You have control over the advertising data collected on your device. Tap Reset advertising ID or Delete advertising ID. By doing this, the ads you see will no longer be targeted based on your previous activity. 7.
